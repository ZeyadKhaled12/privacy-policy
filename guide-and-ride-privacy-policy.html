Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 4, 2025  •  App: Guide & Ride  •  Canonical URL: https://guideandride.net/privacy-policy

1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Guide & Ride (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2) Who uses our app

The app serves tourism operations with four roles:

  • Tourism companies (registration and management)
  • Transport companies
  • Car drivers (single-car operators)
  • Tour guides

3) Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service and meet legal requirements:

A) You provide to us

  • Account details: email, username, phone number, address.
  • Role-related documents:
    • Car photos (for drivers/transport companies)
    • Tour guide certificates/photos (for tour guides)

B) Collected automatically

  • Device & app info: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage data: screens viewed, interactions, and session metadata.
  • Location (if enabled): to support mapping, pickup/drop-off, and assignment features.

C) Android permissions (only when needed)

  • Location (approximate/precise): routing, nearby matching, service zones.
  • Camera / Photos & Videos: capture and upload car photos and certificates.
  • Storage: cache content and enable uploads.
  • Notifications: send status updates you opt into.

4) How we use information

  • Provide and operate app features for all roles.
  • Verify identities and role qualifications (e.g., car ownership, guide certification).
  • Improve quality, safety, and reliability (analytics, crash diagnostics, fraud prevention).
  • Communicate important updates (in-app or push notifications).
  • Comply with legal requirements and enforce terms.

5) Sharing and disclosures

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers (processors) for hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and messaging—under confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Public authorities or third parties when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers in case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, under the same privacy commitments.

6) International transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your country. Where required, we use safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

7) Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above and as required by law, then delete or anonymize it. You can request deletion of your account and data as described below.

8) Security

We apply technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, least-privilege policies). No method is 100% secure; we continuously improve our safeguards.

9) Children’s privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age required by local law (typically 13 or 16). If you believe a child provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10) Your rights and choices

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in EEA/UK; CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port data; object to or restrict processing; and withdraw consent (e.g., for precise location or notifications). To exercise rights, contact us below. You can also:

  • Manage app permissions in Android Settings.
  • Disable push notifications in system settings.

11) Cookies and similar technologies

If we use in-app web views or a website, we may use cookies/local storage for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

12) Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

We rely on: Contract (to provide the service), Legitimate interests (improve, secure, support), Consent (e.g., precise location, notifications), and Legal obligations (compliance).

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post updates at the canonical URL above and revise the effective date. Material changes may be notified in-app.

14) Contact

We currently do not provide an email inbox for privacy requests. Please reach us via the contact form on our website: https://guideandride.net. If you prefer postal mail, include sufficient details for us to identify your account.
